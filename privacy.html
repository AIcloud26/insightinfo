Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April

Your privacy matters. Here's what we collect, how we use it, and how you can control your data. We've made this policy as clear as possible—because you shouldn't need a PhD to understand what happens to your information.

The Short Version

  • We collect minimal data. Just what's needed to run the site.
  • We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to anyone.
  • We use cookies. To make the site work better, not to stalk you.
  • You're in control. You can opt out, delete your data, or contact us anytime.

What Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address (and that's it)
  • Comments/Contact: Name, email, and whatever you choose to share

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit Insightinfo, we automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic location (country/city level, not your address)

Note: This data is aggregated and anonymized. We can't—and don't want to—identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Deliver and improve our content
  • Send newsletters (only if you signed up)
  • Respond to your messages
  • Analyze site traffic and trends
  • Protect against spam and abuse

What we DON'T do: Sell your email, share personal data with third parties for marketing, or track you across the internet.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Essential cookies: Keep the site running (login state, preferences)
  • Analytics cookies: Understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The site will still work, but some features may be limited.

Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand site traffic (you can opt out with the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on)
  • CDN & Hosting: To deliver content quickly and reliably

These services have their own privacy policies. We only share with them what's necessary for them to do their jobs.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Ask us to fix inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time (link in every email)
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a portable format

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption across the entire site
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data (only key team members)

No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Newsletter emails: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: Aggregated after 26 months, anonymized
  • Contact inquiries: Up to 1 year after resolution

Children's Privacy

Insightinfo is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced on our site.

Contact Us

Questions? Concerns? Want to exercise your rights?

  • Email: privacy@insightinfo.com

We aim to respond within 30 days.
